### Card: Case study — Trillions Tracker ⊕
- **Label:** Case study
- **Headline:** The Trillions Tracker for ONE Campaign
- **Detail:** A groundbreaking interactive data visualisation that tracks progress in global financing for People and Planet, developed for the UNGA.
- **Expand — Background:** An estimated US$2.5 trillion annual spending is meant to fund the energy transition, protect people from climate change and meet development needs in low- and middle-income countries. The ONE Campaign needed a way to make this data accessible and hold leaders accountable.
- **Expand — Impact:** We designed an interactive tool that visualises progress across domestic resources, aid, multilateral and bilateral lending. Used by policymakers and advocates to track commitments against reality.
- **Link:** https://www.parabole.studio/project/the-trillions-tracker
