CARD: The Trillions Tracker (cool gray, clickable)
  Label: Case study
  Headline: The Trillions Tracker
  Detail: We developed an interactive data visualisation for ONE Campaign to track progress in global financing for People and Planet, launched at the UNGA.

  POPUP:
    Title: The Trillions Tracker
    Description: We developed an interactive data visualisation for ONE Campaign to track progress in global financing for People and Planet, launched at the UNGA.
    ---
    Background: Tracking global progress on development financing is notoriously messy: different data sources, inconsistent reporting, huge figures with little context. The ONE Campaign spent months consolidating the numbers into a coherent picture and needed a way to present it that could work both on stage at the UN General Assembly and online for a global audience.
    Impact: We designed the full visual experience, from an animated interactive graphic for the web to a printed handout with QR code for UNGA attendees. The Trillions Tracker was presented live at UNGA in September 2024 to mark ONE Campaign's 20th anniversary.

    Title: Strategy first, execution second
    Description: Every project starts with a question: who needs to understand this, and what should change as a result?
    ---
	We split every engagement into two clear stages. First, we define the narrative strategy: the audience, the key message, the right format, the editorial angle. Only once that's agreed do we move into production.
	This is what makes us a strategic partner rather than a production studio. We're in the room when the brief is being shaped, not just when the design is being made. That early involvement is where the most important decisions happen.
